Hatchery Machines Ltd. was founded in 2012 with the goal of producing incubators for the poultry industry that are both hobbyist and commercially oriented. With 11 years of experience, we are aware that investments in the production of poultry increase in value as a result of knowledge, expertise, and high-quality production equipment. Hatchery, breeding, and processing are a few of the numerous projects involved in poultry production.

Hatchery Machines is a leader in all stages of chick production and provides a vast array of products for the incubation, breeding, and processing of chicken, turkey, quail, pheasant, partridge, duck, goose, and ostrich species. We also develop unique solutions specifically for amateurs. Cimuka and HatchTech manufacture a broad line of conventional and unique customized solutions for greater capacity projects, including incubators and hatchers with capacities ranging from 30 to 90,000 eggs. We provide hatchery equipment, such as modular temperature systems, egg storage, and fumigation cabinets, and candling and transfer tables, to support incubation systems based on our client’s demands. All of the equipment can be customized to meet the unique needs of each client.

Achieving predictable, consistent, superior chick quality begins with making sure the needs of each embryo are consistently and uniformly met during incubation. The embryo temperature, determined by the environmental conditions around the egg, is especially important. All of our incubators, setters and hatchers are designed to provide embryos and chicks with optimal, uniform conditions.

With 11 years of experience, we are aware that investments in poultry production has increased in value as a result of knowledge, expertise, and high-quality hatchery equipment. The following technology is what we create for you:

  • DIGIFELfor precise measurement with PID1control,
  • Humusonicfor stable humidity control,
  • Airroutefor optimum temperature and humidity distribution,
  • TurnXfor non-vibrating incubator egg trays,
  • HatcheCO technologies for better and more precise ventilation with high measurements.
